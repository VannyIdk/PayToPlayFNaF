How it started

Five Nights At Freddy's 1 - A horror game based off of it's cretor's nightmare where he was stuck in a office having to defend himself against a robot rabbit using only doors, cameras, and a flashlight. before the game Scott Cawton had some experience with arcade games, tycoons, and other. After his first full game "Tyke and Sons Lumber CO" got critisized and taken off of the internet Five Nights at Freddy's was supposed to be Scott's last project but it got very famous so the franchize continued.

Characters

Bonnie the Bunny - A blue bunny animatronic with a guitar. Activates from the night one, was supposed to be the main Animatronic of the game due to him being in Scott's nightmare but Freddy took his place.

Chica the Chiken - A yellow chiken animatronic with her pal, Carl the cupcake. Active from night 1, is the most underrated character in the franchize and is one of the most aggresive ones in the Movie.

Freddy Fazbear - the main singer of the band, and the main character in the game. Attacks from night 3 and will appear in the right door, is invisible to cameras and can reveal himself by giggling when is close to the player.

Foxy the Pirate - Foxy the pirate Fox is a broken animatronic who sits in his own small stage aka the pirates cave. He attacks from any night if the player doesn't keep an eye on him. He will run to the left door and if isn't closed in time he will kill him.

Golden Freddy/Yellow bear - is a golden version of Freddy, while theres not much information we know about, he is a very important part of the lore and is the one who murdered the Phone Guy according to theories. He appears on 6-th night and crashes the game if encountered and ignored. He is the only one in the game without a animated jumpscare and many people think he or they have 2 souls, Crying Child and Cassidy. Earlier somewhere in 2014-2015 people were saying he is the one who commited the bite of 87 as if in the 6-th night mode if you put 1987 - his jumpscare will appear and crash the game. As the lore was changing Scott Cawton said that now it was a wrong guess and he made the bite of 83. Although everything can happen. So for now we'll just let it be what it is because otherwise we'll accidently touch the lore of FNaF 2, 3, 4, etc.
